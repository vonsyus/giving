We are pioneers in feline genetics, committed to making a positive contribution to animal health and welfare.

We want to help get ALL kitties adopted into forever (happy) homes

If you are a registered nonprofit, you can become a #BasepawsGives partner and receive powerful tools to support your work:

Driving donations:

Basepaws will donate a percentage of sales to your organization

Exposure and brand awareness:

You will be featured in our newsletter, PR and on social media (50k+ reach)

Education:

As a #BasepawsGives partner, you will get exclusive access to our online community, speaking engagements and educational online webinars

Adoption Support:

Basepaws will offer discounted genetic testing for all your kitties, helping you understand each cat’s needs and traits better. We will also feature your adoptable cats on our social media.

Featured Programs & Initiatives

Basepaws donated more than $2,000 to Winn Feline Foundation’s Ricky Fund, supporting research into feline HCM (Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy)

Between March and May 2020, Basepaws donated 100 free DNA tests to cats that were adopted during the pandemic. Read more here.

Basepaws is working closely with Marley’s Mittens, a feline division of Marley’s Mutts, in securing the health and safety of 15+ cats currently in their “cat house”. We raised $500 in an Instagram fundraiser and continue to explore new joint programs, such as the “Pawsitive Change Prison Program”.
